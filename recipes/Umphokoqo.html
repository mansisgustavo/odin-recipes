Umphokoqo

Description

Phutu (Umphokoqo or Umvubo) is a traditional summer dish, especially common to the Xhosa people. It's a type of crumbly pap made of maize meal simmered in water. The cooked porridge is cooled and served with sour milk poured over it.

Ingredients

  • 500 ml water
  • 5 ml salt
  • 3 cups of maize meal

Directions

  1. Bring the water and salt to boil in a pot.
  2. Add the maize meal, and cover with a lid and let it boil.
  3. Once it is boiling, stir with a fork until the water is absorbed. This will create a nice rough texture.
